# Privacy Policy

## Last revised: March 2026

This page outlines how Cida's Kitchen handles user privacy.

Cida's Kitchen does not gather, retain, or distribute personal data of any kind.

No monitoring or identification tools are included in the app, such as cookies, analytics services, or advertising trackers.

All app preferences and saved choices remain exclusively on the user's device and are never sent to external servers.
